Eligibility :

All streams/branches of B.E/B.Tech/M.E/M.Tech, MCA, and M.Sc. (CSE, IT only) from 2022 year of pass outs with full-time education only (Part-time and distance educations are not considered)

  1. Your highest educational qualification only will be considered
  2. Candidate should not have appeared for Accenture Recruitment assessment/interview process in the last three months.
  3. Candidate should not have more than 11 months of experience
  4. Candidates must be eligible to work in India either by citizenship/ have relevant work permit documents.
  5. Please note that Bhutan and Nepal nationals can work in India without a need for obtaining a work visa. All other foreign nationals require work visa or an Overseas Citizenship of India (OCI) or Person of Indian Origin (PIO) card to work in India
  6. You are willing to work in any Business Unit/Service line within the company
  7. You are willing to join /relocate to any Accenture office across India
