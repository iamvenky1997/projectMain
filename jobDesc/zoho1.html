Zoho Recruitment 2022 Hiring Software Engineers With 5 LPA

About company :

ZOHO Corporation is an Indian multinational technology company that makes web-based business tools. It is best known for the online office suite offering Zoho Office Suite. The company was founded in 1996 by Sridhar Vembu and Tony Thomas and has a presence in seven locations with its globalheadquarters in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, and corporate headquarters outside of Austin in Del Valle, Texas. Radha Vembu, Sridhar Vembu’s sister, owns a majority stake in the company. The company was known as AdventNet, Inc. and initially provided network management solutions. AdventNet expanded operations into Japan in 2001 and shifted focus to small and medium businesses.
