About

Hexaware Technologies is an information technology (IT) and business services company based in Navi Mumbai, India. It was founded in 1990 by Atul Nishar. The company has offices in countries across the world, including the United States, Mexico, Canada, Brazil, Sydney in Australia, Singapore, Japan, India, China, United Arab Emirates, United Kingdom, Germany, Romania, and Netherlands.

Services

Hexaware offers a wide variety of specialized business services for various industries, including banking, education, insurance, manufacturing, travel and transportation, capital markets, and more. Hexaware offers services and solutions in application transformation management, business intelligence and analytics, business processes, digital assurance, and enterprise software.
