Jar is hiring for: Frontend Developer

About

Jar is a savings app that helps its users save daily in the form of digital gold — we wish to help Indians rediscover the long-term benefits of habitual daily savings in an era where instant gratification is king.

Jar was founded by Nishchay AG, former director and founding member at mobility start-up Bounce, and Misbah Ashraf, founder of community-focused e-commerce platform Marsplay.

We're looking for driven and enthusiastic individuals. We want you to be comfortable being open to feedback and making rapid improvements; to enable and empower your peers; and finally, we want you to learn. Jar isn't paying you just to work, but also paying you to learn - you're bringing and taking something from the table.

Responsibilities :
  1. Implement Visual Designs clean markup & great fidelity (HTML5/CSS3/JS)
  2. Develop responsive designs and thoroughly test cross browser compatibility of all product interfaces
  3. Identify and research budding UI technologies
  4. Suggest and implement improvements to existing product lines
  5. Brainstorm in building wireframes/prototypes
  6. Make data-driven decisions to improve user engagement
  7. Engage with designers and the backend team to collect & synthesize feedback
  8. Ready to explore and perform tasks that are out of your own pool of expertise
  9. Make pages optimized for both performance as well as SEO
Requirements :
  1. Expert in making scalable and modular Front-End code
  2. In-depth knowledge of HTML, CSS & JavaScript (Typescript is a bonus)
  3. Good experience in making Progressive Web Apps
  4. Knowledge of cross browsers & devices development issues and inconsistencies
  5. Experience in Front end frameworks like React
  6. Experience with building fintech products
  7. A Background in Computer Science
  8. Experience working on Web flow or WordPress
