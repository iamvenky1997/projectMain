Master card is hiring :Software Engineer

About

Mastercard Inc. is the second-largest payment-processing corporation worldwide. It offers a range of financial services. Its headquarters are in Purchase, New York.

Eligibillity criteria
  1. Participate in the gathering of business requirements
  2. Ensure software designs align with MasterCard’s Enterprise Architecture and leverage our enterprise services
  3. Ensure that product development meets MasterCard’s business needs
  4. Ensure that development teams use the appropriate MasterCard standard development methodologies and follow our technology standards
  5. Check that all projects and development are appropriately documented
  6. Ensure that software and system design and delivery support the goal of zero down-time in production
  7. Work closely with business partners on product definition, product roadmaps, business case development and overall direction for the team
  8. Respond to operational and business concerns or issues relative to future business programs, projects, or enhancements to ensure consistent delivery of quality services
  9. Ensure development supports all appropriate certifications and compliance requirements that include those for PCI, GLBA, Safe Harbor and others
  10. Be responsible for performance engineering of the enterprise software architecture and provide functional guidance, advice and/ or training to other application developers
Eligibillity criteria
  1. Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Information Technology, Computer Science or Engineering or equivalent work experience
  2. Excellent Communication skills required to interact across multiple organizational levels of internal and external customer groups
  3. Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities, with quick adaptation to new technologies, methodologies and system
  4. High-energy, detailed-oriented, proactive and able to function under pressure in an independent environment along with a high degree of initiative and self-motivation to drive results
  5. Strong interpersonal, relationship-building with a solid track record for developing internal and external
  6. Proficient in working with J2EE, Java 1.4 or higher, JSP, Servlets, EJB, truts, Spring, Hibernate and Database (Oracle, Exadata, Netezza, SQL Server)
  7. Experience with various tools (GSM, Bamboo/ Maven) and methodologies (Waterfall, Agile)
