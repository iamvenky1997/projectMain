Technical job interviews for positions in the tech industry are generally quite different from regular job interviews. During the job interview, you may be asked a range of questions related to educational aspects, behavioural aspects, situational aspects and technical aspects. Whether you are a fresher or someone who has been in the tech industry for a considerable amount of time, you will definately get benefit from this article .

The purpose of technical interview questions is to assess if your level of technical knowledge is on par with what is required for the position you are interviewing for.

Depending on the company, technical interviews can take place over the phone, via video conferencing online and in person. The interviewing process may be an hour-long or consist of several interview rounds that can take up an entire day. During these time-frames, you can expect to face everything from regular technical proficiency questions to brainteasers and problem-solving questions

Write a code whether given number is prime number or not?

Brute force solution :

if suppose n = 20

You may iterate over from 2 to 19 and check whether given number(20) is divisible by any of the number which in the range of 2 to 20.

count = 0
for i in range (2,n-1):
    if n % i == 0:
        count += 1
        break
if n >1 and count == 0:
    print("it is a prime number")
else:
    print("It is not a prime number")
